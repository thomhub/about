Thom Hubacek Jr, RLA

Landscape Architect

Dedicated, multi-talented and registered Landscape Architect, Planner and Project Manager with more than 25 years of top-flight experience in creating outdoor environments, public parks, community planning, commercial and cooperate business parks and headquarters with sustainable practices including environmental clean-up and reclamation for new developments. Proven record as efficient, thorough manager of resources, finances and people. Able to consistently deliver successful outcomes in rapidly changing, fast-paced environments. Consummate team player who works effectively and professionally with colleagues, management and clientele.

Key Skills Assessment

Landscape and Park Design and Development – Plan, design and implement short-term and long-term strategies with consideration of aesthetics, maintenance, sustainability, environmental responsibility, erosion control, storm water and wastewater management and customer/user satisfaction. Budget and Project Management – Commitment to high standards of budgetary controls and effective supervision and oversight to optimize resources, finances and workforce.
